Cook From Home

Chicken is generally an affordable and accessible meat option for many people, especially compared to other protein sources like red meat or seafood. Chicken is an extremely versatile protein that can be prepared in countless ways - grilled, baked, roasted, fried, etc. This makes it easy to incorporate into a variety of meals. We include step-by-step instructions, cooking techniques, and helpful tips to ensure you can successfully prepare the chicken recipes from home.
